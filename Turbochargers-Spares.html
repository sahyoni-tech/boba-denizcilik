Turbochargers and Spares

The Turbocharger is one of the most important engine component and need quality spares to achieve the most effective and reliable Turbocharger performance.

BOBA specializes in supplying high quality spare parts suitable for various turbocharger brands We deliver from stock or directly from the factory.

All components that leave our workshop are measured, checked and properly packed – ready for shipment. We have a very large inventory of turbochargers and their original parts.

We supply Turbocharger and associated spare parts of the following brands:

  • ABB/ BBC
  • Napier
  • Bannewitz
  • Holset
  • KKK
  • Others types upon request
