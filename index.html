About

Who we are

Boba Denizcilik is part of Pillar Group. It is a group of company working in the field’s construction and MEP works, it was established in 2001 by National Administrative team with international vision, specializing in contracting and infrastructure, providing works with high quality implementation with most integrated, comprehensive, and professional methodologies and systems aiming to be the first in our customers’ reference list.

  • marine services
  • operating ships to buying and selling all types of ships
  • Diesel Engine repair and maintenance

Boba Denizcilik Services is one of the largest importers and exporters of ship engines and ship spare parts in Turkey, Boba Denizcilik Services is one of the leading companies in Turkey specialized in marine maintenance and marine services.
Our services cover all marine business, rom managing and operating ships to buying and selling all types of ships and maintenance of all types of ships and diesel engines of all sizes and ship spare parts and a ships supplier.
Diesel Engine repair and maintenance is one of the core services of Boba Denizcilik, our skilled engineers and mechanics with shipyard, sea going experience know where to look and where to start trouble shooting any engine. we are able to quickly analyze requirements and get down to completing the job and minimize asset downtime for our customers.
