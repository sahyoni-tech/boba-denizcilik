MAN B&W Diesel Engine Spares

The diesel engines from MAN B&W are suitable for the propulsion and on-board power supply of ships as well as for the emergency power supply of stationary plants. Whether two-stroke or four-stroke engines – the MAN B&W product portfolio includes large diesel engines and turbo machines for maritime and stationary applications.


BOBA specialize in suitable spare parts for MAN B&W diesel engines of the following types:

  • KSZ 52/105
  • KSZ 70/125
  • KSZ 70/150
  • KSZ 90/160
  • KZ 52/90
  • KZ 60/105
  • KZ 70/120
